Magic Tome is a Astral-type Ranged Attack Move. It was added in version 1.2 of the game.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Status Effects
  • 3 How to obtain
  • 4 Compatible Species

Description

Hits one target. Lowers the target’s Ranged Defence.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Ranged Defence Down Debuff

How to obtain

Magic Tome may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Magic Tome can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Astral
