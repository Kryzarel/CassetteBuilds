Beast Camouflage is a Beast-type Status Effect Move.

Contents

Description

Changes the type of the user. Automatically used at the start of battle for 0 AP.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Beast Coating Transmutation

How to obtain

Beast Camouflage may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
