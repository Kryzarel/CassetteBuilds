Dual Wield
Miscellaneous
Type
Typeless
Accuracy100%
Use Cost2 AP
CopyableYes
PriorityNormal (0)

Dual Wield is a Typeless Move.

Description

Uses the next move in the list twice in succession, if you have enough AP.


How to obtain

Dual Wield may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
