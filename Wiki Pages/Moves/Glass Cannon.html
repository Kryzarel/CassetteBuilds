Glass Cannon is a Glass-type Ranged Attack Move.

Contents

  • 1 Description
    • 1.1 Additional Information
  • 2 How to obtain
  • 3 Compatible Species

Description

Hits one target. Also damages the user a little.

Additional Information

The self-damage is calculated with 20 power and 1 absolute damage.

How to obtain

Glass Cannon may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Glass Cannon can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Glass
