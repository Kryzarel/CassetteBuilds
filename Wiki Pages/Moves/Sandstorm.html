Sandstorm is a Earth-type Ranged Attack Move.

Description

Hits a whole team. Reduces the targets’ remaining status effect durations by 1.


How to obtain

Sandstorm may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Sandstorm can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Earth
