Type
Astral
Accuracy100%
Use CostPassive
CopyableYes
PriorityNormal (0)

Astral Resistance is a Astral-type passive Move.

Description

Passively reduces incoming Astral-type damage by 50%, and negates any reactions that would occur. Does not affect critical hits.


How to obtain

Astral Resistance may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Astral Resistance can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Astral
