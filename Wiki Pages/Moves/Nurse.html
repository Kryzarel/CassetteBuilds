Type
Typeless
AccuracyUnavoidable
TargetsSingle
Use Cost5 AP
CopyableYes
PriorityNormal (0)

Nurse is a Typeless Move.

Description

Heals one target for 50% of their Max HP.


How to obtain

Nurse may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
