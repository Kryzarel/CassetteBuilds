Type
Beast

Trap Jaw is a Beast-type passive Status Effect Move. It was added in version 1.2 of the game.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Status Effects
  • 3 How to obtain
  • 4 Compatible Species

Description

Automatically used after Bite. Inflicts the target with Evasion Nullified.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Evasion Nullified Debuff

How to obtain

Trap Jaw may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
