Spring-Load is a Plastic-type passive Status Effect Move. It's exclusive to Clocksley's line.

Contents

Description

Automatically gives the user the Spring-Loaded status if they don’t already have it. This turns the user’s melee attack into ranged attacks.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Spring-Loaded Misc

How to obtain

Spring-Load may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
