Fog is a Water-type Status Effect Move.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Status Effects
  • 3 How to obtain
  • 4 Compatible Species

Description

Reduces accuracy of ranged attacks that aren’t Water, Air or Ice-type to 0% for 3 turns.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Fog Debuff

How to obtain

Fog (move) may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
