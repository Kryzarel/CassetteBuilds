Gear Shear is a Metal-type Ranged Attack Move. It is needed in order to remaster a Velocirifle into a Gearyu.

Description

Hits one target multiple times, with reduced priority compared with other moves.


How to obtain

Gear Shear may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Gear Shear can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Metal

Gear Shear can also be found in a chest located in the cave on the beach north of Autumn Hill.
