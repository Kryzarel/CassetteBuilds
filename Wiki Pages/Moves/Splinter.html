Splinter is a Plant-type passive Melee Attack Move.

Contents

  • 1 Description
    • 1.1 Additional Information
  • 2 How to obtain
  • 3 Compatible Species

Description

Chance to hurt an opponent automatically upon melee contact. Damage is based on the user’s Melee Defence.

Additional Information

Splinter has a 50% chance of activating upon melee contact.

How to obtain

Splinter may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Splinter can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Plant
