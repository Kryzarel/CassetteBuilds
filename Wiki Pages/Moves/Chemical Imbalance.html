Chemical Imbalance is a Poison-type passive Move.

Contents

  • 1 Description
    • 1.1 Additional Information
  • 2 How to obtain
  • 3 Compatible Species

Description

Chance to automatically use the next move in the list, for 0 AP, when the user lands a type-advantaged hit. The chance is reduced in proportion to the move’s normal AP cost.

Additional Information

The chance that Chemical Imbalance will activate is determined by the following formula.

Chemical Imbalance may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
