Miscellaneous
Type
Beast
AccuracyUnavoidable
TargetsSingle Ally (Not Self)
Use Cost2 AP
CopyableYes
PriorityNormal (0)

Blood Donation is a Beast-type Move.

Description

Reduces the user’s HP by half, giving it to the target.


How to obtain

Blood Donation may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Blood Donation can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Beast
