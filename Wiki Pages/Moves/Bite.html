Bite is a Beast-type Melee Attack Move.

Contents

  • 1 Description
    • 1.1 Additional Information
  • 2 How to obtain
  • 3 Compatible Species

Description

Hits one target. Heals the user a little bit afterwards.

Additional Information

  • Heals the user by 25% of the damage dealt to the target. Having Carnivore equipped doubles the healing amount to 50%
  • Automatically triggers Trap Jaw after use if equipped.

How to obtain

Bite may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Bite can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Beast
