Sharp Edges is a Typeless Status Effect Move.

Contents

    • 1.1 Additional Information
  • 2 Status Effects
  • 3 How to obtain
  • 4 Compatible Species

Description

Gives the user a Contact Damage status for 3 turn(s).

Additional Information

The effect given will match the elemental type of the user. For fusion monsters with more than one type, the first type is used.

How to obtain

Sharp Edges may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Sharp Edges can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Glass
  • Metal
