Self-Assured
Miscellaneous
Type
Typeless
Accuracy70%
Use CostPassive
CopyableYes
PriorityNormal (0)

Self-Assured is a Typeless passive Move.

Description

Prevents the user from being inflicted with Confused.


How to obtain

Self-Assured may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
