Hot Potato is a Fire-type Status Effect Move.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Status Effects

Description

Places a bomb on the target that will explode if not immediately passed on by melee contact.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Bomb Debuff

How to obtain

Hot Potato may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
