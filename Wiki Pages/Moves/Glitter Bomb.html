Glitter Bomb is a Glitter-type Ranged Attack Move.

Description

Hits one target.


How to obtain

Glitter Bomb may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Glitter Bomb can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Glitter
