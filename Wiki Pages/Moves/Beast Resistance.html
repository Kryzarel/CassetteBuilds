Beast Resistance is a Beast-type passive Move.

Description

Passively reduces incoming Beast-type damage by 50%, and negates any reactions that would occur. Does not affect critical hits.


How to obtain

Beast Resistance may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Beast Resistance can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Beast
