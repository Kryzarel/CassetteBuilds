Shield Bash is a Metal-type Melee Attack Move.

Description

Hits one target. Uses the user’s Melee Defence stat instead of Melee Attack.


How to obtain

Shield Bash may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Shield Bash can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Metal
