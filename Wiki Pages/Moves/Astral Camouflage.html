Astral Camouflage is a Astral-type Status Effect Move.

Contents

  • 2 Status Effects

Description

Changes the type of the user. Automatically used at the start of battle for 0 AP.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Astral Coating Transmutation

How to obtain

Astral Camouflage may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
