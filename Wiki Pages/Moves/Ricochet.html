Type
Typeless
Accuracy100%
Use CostPassive
CopyableYes

Ricochet is a Typeless passive Move.

Description

The user’s ranged attacks have a 50% chance to ignore opponents’ walls.


How to obtain

Ricochet may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
