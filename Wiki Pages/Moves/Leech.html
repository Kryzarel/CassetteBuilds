Leech is a Plant-type Status Effect Move.

Contents

Description

Gives the target Leeched status for 3 turn(s).

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Leeched Debuff

How to obtain

Leech may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Leech can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Plant
