Fire

Num Hits1

CopyableYes

Guzzle Fuel is a Fire-type Move.

Description

Removes 2 AP from the target.


How to obtain

Guzzle Fuel may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Guzzle Fuel can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Fire
