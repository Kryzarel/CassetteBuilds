Plastic Knife is a Plastic-type Melee Attack Move.

Description

Hits one target multiple times.


How to obtain

Plastic Knife may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Plastic Knife can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Plastic
