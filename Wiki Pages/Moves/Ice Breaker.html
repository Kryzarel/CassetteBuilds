Ice Breaker is a Ice-type Melee Attack Move.

Description

Hits one target. Lowers the target’s Speed.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Speed Down Debuff

How to obtain

Ice Breaker may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Ice Breaker can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Ice
