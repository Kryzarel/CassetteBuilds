Cold Fusion is a Ice-type passive Move. It was added in version 1.2 of the game.

Description

Activates whenever anyone fuses. Reduces the fusion’s HP by 20% of its Max HP, and causes it to flinch.


How to obtain

Cold Fusion may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Cold Fusion can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Ice
