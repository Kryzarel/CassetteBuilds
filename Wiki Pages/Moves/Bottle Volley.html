Bottle Volley is a Glass-type Ranged Attack Move.

Description

Hits one target multiple times.


How to obtain

Bottle Volley may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Bottle Volley can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Glass
