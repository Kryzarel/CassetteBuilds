Pustule Bomb is a Poison-type Ranged Attack Move.

Description

Hits a whole team.

Additional Information

Pustule Bomb has a 100% chance of causing poison.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Poisoned Debuff

How to obtain

Pustule Bomb may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Pustule Bomb can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Poison
