Type
Glass
Accuracy100%
TargetsSingle
Use Cost2 AP
CopyableYes
PriorityNormal (0)

Revolving Door is a Glass-type Move.

Description

Steals the target’s wall and transfers it to the user.


How to obtain

Revolving Door may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Revolving Door can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Glass
