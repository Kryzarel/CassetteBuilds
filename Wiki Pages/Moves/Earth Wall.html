Earth Wall is a Earth-type Status Effect Move.

Description

Sacrifice 20% of HP to create a wall that will absorb up to 3 hits for up to 3 turns.

Earth Wall Buff

How to obtain

Earth Wall (move) may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
