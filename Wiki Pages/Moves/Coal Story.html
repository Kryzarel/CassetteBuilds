Coal Story is a Earth-type Ranged Attack Move.

Description

Hits one target. Inflicts Burned on the target.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Burned Debuff

How to obtain

Coal Story may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Coal Story can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Earth
  • Fire
