Hibernate is a Astral-type Status Effect Move. It's exclusive to Stardigrade's line.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Status Effects

Description

Puts the user to sleep. The user recovers HP every turn it’s asleep.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Hibernating Buff
Sleep Debuff

How to obtain

Hibernate may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
