Description

Hits one target. Poisons the target.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Poisoned Debuff

How to obtain

Toxic Stab may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Toxic Stab can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Poison
