Metal Riff is a Metal-type Ranged Attack Move.

Description

Hits a whole team. Becomes an Air-type attack when equipped to a Metal-type tape.


How to obtain

Metal Riff may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Metal Riff can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Metal
