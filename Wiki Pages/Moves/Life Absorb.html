Life Absorb is a Plant-type Melee Attack Move.

Description

Recovers an amount of HP equal to the damage dealt.


How to obtain

Life Absorb may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Life Absorb can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Plant
