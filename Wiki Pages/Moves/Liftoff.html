Type
Air
Accuracy100%
Use CostPassive
CopyableYes
PriorityNormal (0)

Liftoff is a Air-type passive Status Effect Move. It was added in version 1.5 of the game.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Status Effects
  • 3 How to obtain
  • 4 Compatible Species

Description

Gives the user AP Boost and Speed Up at the end of their third turn.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
AP Boost Buff
Speed Up Buff

How to obtain

Liftoff may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
