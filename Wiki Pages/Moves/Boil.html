Boil is a Water-type Melee Attack Move.

    • 1.1 Additional Information

Description

Hits one target. Chance to burn the target.

Additional Information

Boil has a 50% chance of causing a burn.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Burned Debuff

How to obtain

Boil may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Boil can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Fire
  • Water
