Yule Regret That is a Ice-type Ranged Attack Move. It's exclusive to Elfless' line.

Description

Hits one target. Lands a critical hit if the target has already taken damage this turn.


How to obtain

Yule Regret That may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
