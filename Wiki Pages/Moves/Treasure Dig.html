Treasure Dig is a Earth-type Move. It was added in version 1.2 of the game. It’s exclusive to Picksie.

Description

Digs up a random item. The winner of the battle gets to keep the item.

Additional Information

If successful, Treasure Dig will generate one random item from the low-level loot table if the user is at a higher level than the opponent, or from the high-level loot table if at least one opponent has the same or higher level as the user.
