Sticky Tongue is a Beast-type Melee Attack Move.

Description

Hits one target. Lowers the target’s Evasion.

Additional Information

Lowers the target's evasion by 4 stages via Evasion Down.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Evasion Down Debuff

How to obtain

Sticky Tongue may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
