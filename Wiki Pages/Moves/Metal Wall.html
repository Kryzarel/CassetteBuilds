Metal

Metal Wall is a Metal-type Status Effect Move.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Status Effects
  • 3 How to obtain
  • 4 Compatible Species

Description

Sacrifice 20% of HP to create a wall that will absorb up to 3 hits for up to 3 turns.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Metal Wall Buff

How to obtain

Metal Wall (move) may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
