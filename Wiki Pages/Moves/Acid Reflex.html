Acid Reflex is a Poison-type passive Status Effect Move. It's exclusive to Jellyton.

Description

Chance to poison an opponent automatically upon melee contact.

Additional Information

Acid Reflex has a 50% chance of causing poison upon melee contact.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Poisoned Debuff

How to obtain

Acid Reflex may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
