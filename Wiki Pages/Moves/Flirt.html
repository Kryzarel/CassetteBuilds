Flirt is a Typeless Status Effect Move.

Description

Lowers the target’s Evasion.

Additional Information

Lowers the target's evasion by 4 stages via Evasion Down.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Evasion Down Debuff

How to obtain

Flirt may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
