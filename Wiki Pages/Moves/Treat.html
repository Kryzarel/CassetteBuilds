Treat is a Typeless Status Effect Move.

    • 1.1 Additional Information

Description

Gives the target a random buff.

Additional Information

In addition to the listed Status Effects, Treat can also give the target an Elemental Wall or Contact Damage that matches the user's type.

How to obtain

Treat may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
