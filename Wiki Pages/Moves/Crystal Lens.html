Crystal Lens is a Glass-type Ranged Attack Move.

Description

Hits one target.


How to obtain

Crystal Lens may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Crystal Lens can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Glass
  • Ice
