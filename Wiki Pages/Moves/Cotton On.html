Cotton On is a Plant-type Status Effect Move.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Status Effects
  • 3 How to obtain

Description

Reduces the accuracy of moves that can be evaded that target the user to 0% for the next round.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Cottoned On Buff

How to obtain

Cotton On may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
