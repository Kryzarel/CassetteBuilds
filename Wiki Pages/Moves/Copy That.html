Copy That
Miscellaneous
Type
Air
AccuracyUnavoidable
TargetsSingle
Use Cost2 AP
CopyableYes
PriorityNormal (0)

Copy That is a Air-type Move.

Description

Transforms the user into a copy of the target.


How to obtain

Copy That may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
