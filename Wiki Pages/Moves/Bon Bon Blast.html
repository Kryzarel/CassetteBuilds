Bon Bon Blast is a Typeless Ranged Attack Move. It's exclusive to Candevil's line.

Description

Hits one target. Lowers the target’s Evasion.

Additional Information

Lowers the target's evasion by 4 stages via Evasion Down.

Status Effects

Status EffectKind
Evasion Down Debuff

How to obtain

Bon Bon Blast may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.
