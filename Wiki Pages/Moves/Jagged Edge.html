Jagged Edge is a Typeless passive Melee Attack Move.

Description

Automatically inflicts damage when a melee attack misses the user.


How to obtain

Jagged Edge may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Jagged Edge can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Glass
  • Ice
  • Metal
