Undertow is a Water-type Ranged Attack Move.

Description

Hits the last fighter to take their turn before this move.


How to obtain

Undertow may be obtained when a compatible tape levels up.

Undertow can be obtained when a bootleg of one of the following types levels up:

  • Water
